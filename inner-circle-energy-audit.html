How to Do It

This exercise helps you become more intentional about the people and influences in your life. You'll rate each person or influence across six categories to understand their impact on your energy and growth.

Rating Scale: 0 (drains me) to 5 (empowers me)

Rate each person or influence across these 6 categories:

  • Money / Ambition: Do they inspire financial growth or ambitious thinking?
  • Emotional Support: Do they provide genuine care and emotional safety?
  • Life Example: Is their lifestyle something you admire or want to model?
  • Learning / Growth: Do you learn valuable skills or perspectives from them?
  • Fun / Energy: Do they bring joy, laughter, and positive energy?
  • Trust: Can you be vulnerable and authentic with them?
Reflection: After completing your audit, notice who scores high (20+) and who scores low. This isn't about cutting people out—it's about understanding where to invest your time and energy.

When to Do This Exercise

Do this audit when you feel drained, stuck, or disconnected from your goals. It's especially powerful during life transitions—starting a new career, ending a relationship, or feeling like you've outgrown your current circle. Repeat it every 6-12 months to track how your relationships evolve.

This is not a one-time exercise. Your inner circle should evolve as you grow. The people who served you well in one season may not align with the next chapter of your life—and that's okay.

Important Don'ts

  • Don't judge or blame people — awareness is not rejection. Someone who scores low in one area might still be important in others. This is about understanding, not condemnation.
  • Don't rush to 'cut' people — just adjust your distance and energy. A low score doesn't mean you must remove someone completely—it means you might need better boundaries or less frequent contact.
  • Don't forget digital influences — your phone feeds your mind. Include social media accounts, podcasts, and content creators you consume regularly. They're part of your inner circle too.

Our Mission

We aim to instill the importance and philosophy from transformative works such as Think and Grow Rich, Transurfing Reality, and Schoenfield's Busting Loose from the Money Game — specifically, how they unlock the spirit and capability of the human being.

Guiding Human Potential

My greatest goal with COE is to guide and unlock human potential. To preserve and spread the ancient teachings and be a moral compass and belief system that strengthens humanity.

Unified Education

Creating a hub where children can dedicate the same passion they tend to dedicate towards religion, into something that rewards them with practical life skills.

Measurable Outcomes

Where spirituality isn't just a place to 'feel' but actually develop as a human to your full potential — with measurable, real-world results.

Holistic Development

Combining spirituality, elementary education, and higher education for career readiness into one transformative experience.

Our Vision

We are excited that you're thinking about and inspired to join us in generational change and renewal. Together, we're creating a world where human potential isn't just discovered — it's systematically developed, nurtured, and unleashed.

The COE Approach

This concept has been inspired by the idea of a spiritual organization that focuses on delivering useful education in managing the world around you. We don't need separate places for spirituality, elementary education, and higher education for career readiness.

A New Paradigm

Rather than fragmenting human development, we create a unified approach where:

  • Spirituality becomes practical: Not just feeling spiritual, but developing measurable capabilities
  • Education becomes transformative: Learning skills that unlock potential rather than just information
  • Growth becomes systematic: Clear pathways from childhood through adult mastery
  • Results become visible: Measurable outcomes that demonstrate real transformation
